HOSTEL ADMISSION PRIORITY

Admission priority:

1. All children of war widows and widows of ex-servicemen
2. Children studying in Post Graduate (Vocational), Medical, Engineering courses
3. B.A.E., D.A.E.
4. Degree Course (as per Merit List)
5. 12th, 11th, 10th in order (as per quality list)
6. Second and third shifts of ex-servicemen
7. Children of serving soldiers
8. Civilian child if space available (with Director's permission)
